AI Sponsor

Lean Canvas

May 2026 · Review: Monday call with Matt

01

Problem

  • Human sponsors aren't always available — people need support at 3am, during cravings, in crisis
  • Finding and keeping a human sponsor is hard — unpredictable, burnout is real, millions have none
  • Shame makes people hold back even with a human sponsor — anonymity removes the barrier
Existing Alternatives
  • Human sponsors — limited, unpredictable, unpaid
  • Recovery meetings — community, not 1-on-1 or 24/7
  • Therapy — expensive, not recovery-specific
  • ChatGPT — not trained in 12-step culture or steps
02

Solution

  • AI sponsor trained on 12 steps, traditions, and all 10 programs
  • Available via WhatsApp, SMS, or web chat — where sponsors already live
  • Personalised from onboarding: program, stage, goals, sponsor name + style
  • Crisis protocol built in — 988, SAMHSA, program hotline
  • Anonymity-first: first name + last initial only
