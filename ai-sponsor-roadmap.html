Key Risks & Mitigations
Risk
Audos bugs delay launch past beta weekend
Mitigation: VIP bug report submitted to support@audos.com. Irfan on standby. Landing page + form already live.
Risk
Beta users have a bad first experience
Mitigation: Test full user journey end-to-end before Matt's weekend call.
Risk
AI says something harmful in a crisis
Mitigation: Crisis protocol hard-coded in master prompt. 988 + SAMHSA + program hotline triggered automatically.
Risk
Recovery community rejects the product
Mitigation: Honest framing, program disclaimer, 1-for-1 model, no claims of replacing human sponsors.
Risk
Low trial-to-paid conversion
Mitigation: 30-day trial builds habit before card is charged. Card saved day 1, charged day 31.
Risk
Product feels like a chatbot, not a sponsor
Mitigation: Deep program knowledge + sponsor persona in master prompt. Personalised from minute one via onboarding.
