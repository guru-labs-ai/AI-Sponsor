What It Is

AI Sponsor is a 24/7 AI-powered recovery companion designed to support people in 12-step programs whenever they need guidance, accountability, encouragement, or someone to talk to. It acts as an always-available digital sponsor — accessible through WhatsApp — providing immediate emotional support, recovery reminders, relapse interruption, step guidance, check-ins, and structured accountability rooted in 12-step methodologies.

The core value proposition is accessibility: many people in recovery struggle during moments when their human sponsor is unavailable, asleep, busy, or emotionally exhausted. AI Sponsor fills that gap — offering immediate, stigma-free support at 2am during a craving, an emotional spiral, or a moment of uncertainty.

It is not designed to replace human sponsors or recovery communities — it reinforces and extends them. The site includes a clear disclaimer: not affiliated with any 12-step programs. This is a private project.

The 1-for-1 Model — Front and Center

Every paying subscriber ($5/month) automatically gives one free access to someone who can't afford it. This is Matt's core idea — inspired by Tom's Shoes — and it's prominently featured on the landing page and onboarding flow.

On top of that: 1 referral = 1 free month. The product is designed to grow through the recovery community itself, not just paid ads.
