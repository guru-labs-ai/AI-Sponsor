What It Is

AI Sponsor is a 24/7 AI-powered recovery companion for people in 12-step programs. It's a conversational chatbot, powered by Claude, that a person reaches on WhatsApp (or web chat). The user always starts the conversation — they message whenever they need guidance, encouragement, step work, or someone to talk to at 2am during a craving — and the sponsor responds in context, remembering who they are.

The core value proposition is accessibility: many people in recovery struggle in moments when their human sponsor is unavailable, asleep, or busy. AI Sponsor fills that gap with immediate, stigma-free, always-on support — across every program, not just alcohol.

It reinforces human sponsors and meetings — it does not replace them. The site carries a clear disclaimer: not affiliated with any 12-step program. This is a private project.

User Journey — What Actually Happens

Primary path — WhatsApp (open model):

1. A person discovers AI Sponsor (beta group, referral, ad, or AI-search result).
2. They message the WhatsApp number (our dedicated Twilio number).
3. The sponsor (Claude) replies right away — warm, asks what's going on. No code, no gate.
4. The conversation continues — text or voice notes; the sponsor remembers their name, program, and story.
5. Behind the scenes, the contact is captured in GoHighLevel (CRM).
6. They come back whenever they need support, 24/7 — they start it every time.

Web path: land on getaisponsor.com → register (9-screen onboarding: program, recovery stage, what brought them, goals, sponsor name/style) → a sponsor personalized to their answers → chat, or directed to WhatsApp. Beta users = free (code/tag); public = 30-day trial → $5/mo.

User Stories
As someone in recovery, I want to reach a sponsor at 3am, so I get support the moment I need it — not whenever a human is free.
As a newcomer who can't find a human sponsor, I want immediate guidance, so I don't white-knuckle it alone.
As someone in a non-alcohol program (GA, DA, OA, SLAA…), I want a sponsor who knows my program, so I feel understood — not redirected to drinking advice.
As a returning user, I want it to remember my name and story, so I don't have to repeat myself each time.
As a user mid-craving, I want to send a voice note and get a voice reply, so I can talk it out hands-free.
As someone who can't afford it, I want free access funded by someone else's subscription (1-for-1), so cost is never the barrier.

🚫 What AI Sponsor Does NOT Do

• It does NOT send daily reminders, scheduled check-ins, or any outbound/marketing messages. It is user-initiated — the person always starts the conversation.

• It runs on WhatsApp via Twilio (WhatsApp Senders) — NOT SMS / A2P 10DLC. No "Reply STOP," no "msg & data rates," no keyword opt-in flow.

• There is no code gate on WhatsApp (open model). Beta codes only relate to free access on the website.

The 1-for-1 Model — Front and Center

Every paying subscriber ($5/month) automatically gives one free access to someone who can't afford it. This is Matt's core idea — inspired by Tom's Shoes — and it's prominently featured on the landing page and onboarding flow.

On top of that: 1 referral = 1 free month. The product is designed to grow through the recovery community itself, not just paid ads.
